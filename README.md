### True Price Contribution â€” Close the Gap, Make a Difference

- Climate compensation through verified carbon offset programs
- Water restoration projects

- Fair wage programs for farmers and workers

**The more you contribute, the higher your Impact Score â€” and the more rewards you earn.**
